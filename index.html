Beatrice Franzolini

franzolini@pm.me

I am a final year PhD student in Statistics at Bocconi University in Milan, Italy. I am working under the supervision of Prof. Antonio Lijoi and Prof. Igor Prünster.
I am a member of the BayesLab at the Bocconi Institute for Data Science and Analytics ( BIDSA) and member of the Complex Data Modeling Research Network.
My research interests are in Bayesian nonparametrics, Bayesian modelling of complex data and biostatistics.
